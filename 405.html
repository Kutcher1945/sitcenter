Development of a Model for Predicting the Path of a Miner Underground

Company selling miner localization systems to mining companies

An algorithm was developed to predict miner behavior in a mine using statistical models for quick finding of miners in case of emergencies and probable real-time localization for safety.

The developed model significantly increased safety levels in mines, providing the ability to respond quickly in case of emergencies. The probability of successfully finding a miner in an emergency increased by 70%, reducing rescue operation times and minimizing risks to miners' lives.
