Инновационные решения для города

Цифровая платформа Ситуационный центр - это инновационное решение, предназначенное для повышения эффективности управления городскими ресурсами и улучшения качества жизни граждан. Development of an economic diversification strategy

Development of tools and strategies to improve the structure of the economy of the Central Asian country through deepening technological diversification and strengthening export potential.

The project provided government agencies with effective analytical tools to support informed economic policy decisions that promote technological renewal and economic growth.

Research on the impact of the global health crisis on the economy

Analysis of the impact of the pandemic on economic processes in Central Asia, in order to develop strategic solutions to minimize economic losses.

The project provided the customer with in-depth analytical data and forecasts that helped in formulating effective strategies to mitigate the effects of the pandemic on the economy.

Forecasting and research of the construction materials market

Analysis and forecasting of the development of the construction materials market in Central Asia to optimize government support for the industry.

The information provided by the project helped the customer to plan strategic measures to stimulate the growth and sustainability of the industry.

Analysis of mortgage lending and state support programs for housing construction

Assessment of the availability and effectiveness of mortgage lending and government programs to support housing construction in Central Asia.

The analytics developed during the project became the basis for optimizing banking products and government programs aimed at improving the living conditions of the population.

Development of Cloud Architecture for Seamless Integration of Machine Learning Models with the Cloud

Developed an architecture using Terraform (IaC) for 10 different machine learning models with a more reliable storage and access system, offering high computational power.

The bank received a flexible and scalable cloud architecture, significantly improving the performance of machine learning models. The deployment time for new models was reduced by 50%, and integration with cloud services significantly reduced operational costs.

Development of a Model for Predicting the Path of a Miner Underground

Developed an algorithm to predict miner behavior in the mine using statistical models for quick localization in case of emergencies and real-time probable localization for safety.

The developed model significantly improved safety levels in mines, enabling quick response in emergency situations. The probability of successfully finding a miner in case of an emergency increased by 70%, reducing rescue operation times and minimizing risk to miners' lives.

Fraud Detection System

Our web service is designed for effective detection and prevention of anti-spoofing, providing reliable protection against fraudulent attempts and forgery. Used advanced algorithms and machine learning models based on comprehensive data analysis to detect and block unauthorized access attempts and forgery.

The implementation of the system allowed banks to reduce fraud levels by 85% thanks to the high-accuracy detection and prevention system.
